WELCOME TOCENTURY PEST CONTROL, INC.

Century Pest Control, Inc. is a professional pest management business that services residential and commercial properties throughout South Texas. Our main office provides services in Bexar county, the Greater San Antonio Area and surrounding cities. Our branch office provides services to Caldwell county, Lockhart, San Marcos and Lulling.

Since 1974 we have continued to provide solutions to the toughest pest and wildlife issues that have stricken families and businesses in all these areas. Century has stayed in stride with our ever-evolving industry and delivers to you proven pest management strategies crafted by the most current industry standards of today. Our line of materials we use consist of organic formulations, environmentally friendly materials and product choices that are much safer to be applied in your environment.

Century Pest Control takes great steps in ensuring safety for your family, pets, employees, customers and property. All products are registered by the Environmental Protection Agency and are applied in strict accordance with their labeling. Our technicians are licensed and certified by the Texas Department of Agriculture and are rigorously trained to perform a safe pest management service ensuring quality and satisfaction to you our valued customer.

For over 40 years the Angelini family has stood behind all the work of Century Pest Control, Inc. ensuring our constant commitment of delivering top quality service to all our customers.
